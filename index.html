Biography

I am a Ph.D. course student at Yonsei University, working with Prof. Shin in MIDaS. My major is applied mathematics and my research interests are computer graphics, data analysis and deep learning. I have expertise in machine learning and understand different deep learning algorithms such as convolutional neural networks (CNN) and generative adversarial networks (GAN). Besides, I am skilled in deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow and Keras. Recently, I focus on the topic "graph learning" to solve problems in real life.
